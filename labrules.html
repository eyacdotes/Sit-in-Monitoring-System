Welcome to the Lab Sitin Rules!

Follow the rules and enjoy your time here.

Lab Sitin Rules:

  1. No food or drinks allowed.
  2. Keep noise level low.
  3. Respect others' belongings.
  4. Use equipment responsibly.
  5. Do not tamper with experiments.
  6. Leave workstations tidy.
  7. Follow safety protocols.
  8. No unauthorized experiments.
  9. Ask for assistance if needed.
  10. No unauthorized access to computers.
  11. Respect lab staff's instructions.
  12. No smoking inside or near the lab.

Consequences of Violating Rules:

  • First offense: Warning.
  • Second offense: Temporary suspension from lab.
